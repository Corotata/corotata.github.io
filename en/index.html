Professional Textile & Garment
Integrated Enterprise

Sichuan Yaoye Textile Co., Ltd. is located in Tuzhu Textile Park, Leshan City, Sichuan Province, only 10 kilometers away from the world-famous Leshan Giant Buddha. We are an integrated enterprise combining home textiles, clothing, textile industrial research and sales. The company covers an area of 18,981 square meters with a building area of 21,800 square meters, producing 500,000 sets of school uniforms and 400,000 sets of bedding annually, with an annual output value of over 80 million yuan.

Company Profile

Sichuan Yaoye Textile Co., Ltd. has its own well-known brand, with products enjoying a good reputation both at home and abroad.

Quality Assurance

Our company adheres to product quality as our lifeline, carefully selecting raw materials, designing meticulously, providing dedicated service, and operating with integrity to provide consumers with environmentally friendly, healthy, and comfortable home textile and clothing products.

Corporate Vision

We aim to fulfill consumers' aspirations for a better life as our corporate purpose, and strive to create a first-class well-known brand and lead the trend of home textiles and clothing as our goal.

Honors & Achievements

The company has successively won numerous honors such as AAA-level Chinese Quality Credit Enterprise, Sichuan Famous and Quality Product, Sichuan Consumer Satisfaction Product, Sichuan Consumer Trusted Product, Contract-honoring and Trustworthy Enterprise, Green Environmental Protection and Health Product, and Leshan Well-known Trademark.

Corporate Culture

Yaoye always adheres to the business philosophy of 'establishing brand with quality, winning trust with service, and exploring the future with innovation.'

Scientific Management

The company adheres to scientific management and continuous innovation. It has successively passed ISO9001 quality system certification, ISO14001 environmental system certification, five-star after-sales service evaluation system certification, and occupational health and safety management system certification, providing scientific guarantees for the healthy development and standardized operation of the enterprise.

Technological Innovation

We have successively introduced advanced intelligent facilities and equipment such as "automatic cutting bed system", "intelligent hanging system", and "antibacterial disinfection system" to bring better quality products and services to customers.

Social Responsibility

While maintaining stable development, the company never forgets to give back to society. We actively help poor villages develop collective economy, provide on-the-job training and employment positions for migrant workers and disabled people, bringing jobs to their doorsteps, and regularly carry out donation activities to impoverished areas, being an enterprise with love, warmth, and social responsibility.
